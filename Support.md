# BrainWorkout Support

Welcome to the BrainWorkout support page. If you have a question, ran into an issue, or just want to send us feedback, you're in the right place.

## About BrainWorkout

BrainWorkout is a collection of quick brain-training mini-games designed to sharpen memory, focus, and reaction time: Number Sequence, Card Matching, Stroop Challenge, and Schulte Table.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I reset my high scores?**
Open Settings (the gear icon on the main screen), scroll to the high scores section, and tap "Reset High Scores." This clears your best results for every game and cannot be undone.

**Why do I see ads in the app?**
BrainWorkout is free to play and supported by ads, which let us keep building and maintaining the app at no cost to you. You may occasionally see a prompt asking whether we can show you personalized ads — you can allow or decline this at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

**How does the daily streak work?**
Your streak increases by one each calendar day you complete a game. If a full day passes without playing, the streak resets. Streaks are tracked using UTC time to keep day boundaries consistent no matter where you are.

**What does Advanced Mode do?**
Advanced Mode (in Settings) increases the difficulty across all four games — for example, fewer lives in Card Matching, and faster timers in Number Sequence, Stroop Challenge, and Schulte Table.

**My high scores or streak disappeared. What happened?**
Your progress is stored locally on your device and is not backed up to any account or cloud service. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, or restored your device from a backup that predates your progress, that data can't be recovered. We don't have access to it on our end either, since it never leaves your device.

**The app is crashing or a game won't load. What should I do?**
Try force-quitting BrainWorkout and reopening it, and make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store. If the problem continues, contact us below with your device model and iOS version so we can look into it.

**I don't want to be tracked for ads. How do I opt out?**
Go to your device's Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and turn off tracking for BrainWorkout (or turn off "Allow Apps to Request to Track" globally). You'll still see ads, just not personalized ones.
